Alignment

Carlo's Bakary

carlosbakery.com

The arrangement of elements is in a visually cohesive and balanced manner, ensuring that they are properly positioned in relation to each other and the overall layout.

Contrast

Tesla

tesla.com

The deliberate use of differences in colors, shapes, sizes, textures, or other visual elements to create visual interest and emphasize important elements within a design.

Repetition

Ray-Ban

ray-ban.com/usa

The intentional use of recurring visual elements, such as colors, shapes, patterns, or fonts, to create a sense of unity and consistency throughout a design. It helps establish a visual rhythm and reinforces the overall theme or message.
